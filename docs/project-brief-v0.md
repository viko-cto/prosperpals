# ProsperPals — Project Brief v0

## Vision
Build the most emotionally intelligent financial companion for Gen Z.

## Problem
Current finance apps optimize for ledgers, not humans. Users churn because:
- overwhelming setup
- shame-based tone
- broken trust when data or suggestions feel wrong

## Target Users
- Gen Z students and early professionals
- first salary / first rent / debt anxiety phase
- high social media fluency, low patience for corporate UX

## Proposed Solution
ProsperPals introduces two AI companions:
- **Goldie**: encouragement, momentum, wins
- **Fin**: structure, realism, guardrails

Together they deliver tiny daily actions, emotional support, and practical money progress.

## Success Metrics (MVP)
- D7 retention > 35%
- 4+ weekly check-ins per active user
- 60% users set at least one goal in first 72h
- 25% upgrade intent from active cohort

## Constraints
- privacy-first
- clear AI transparency
- explainable suggestions
- non-judgmental language

## Open Questions
- initial geographies for open banking coverage
- premium pricing test bands
- social features at MVP or post-MVP
